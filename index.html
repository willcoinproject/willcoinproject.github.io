What is Willcoin

Our goal is to automate the asset distribution to an individual's beneficiaries when they pass away using Smart Living Wills implemented through smart contracts. Upon passing, an individual's Smart Will automatically executes and assets are transferred to their designated beneficiaries. This allows individuals to skip the expensive and arduous court probate process.

Smart Living Wills

Willcoin leverages smart contract technology to create self-executing Smart Living Wills that live on a distributed network allowing individuals to save on legal fees.

Automatic Asset Distribution

Willcoin helps individuals automatically distribute their personal assets to their designated beneficiaries anywhere in the world while decreasing undesired alterations.

Simplified Probate Process

Smart Wills help the deceased perform the actions they planned upon their death. This removes the need for court probate processes, removes loopholes, and human error commonly found in written Wills.

Technical Details

The idea for Willcoin was born from a personal experience. The father of one of our team members had passed and designated that his assets be transferred to his children. The process should have been a simple one, however due to some ambiguous wording in some parts of his Will, it instead turned into a tedious and expensive seven-month process. The family had to pay expensive court, attorney, bond, business valuation, and other miscellaneous fees. Instead of grieving the passing of their father, the family had to waste time and money in court. We thought this wasn't right so we made Willcoin allowing individuals and their families to find peace knowing that an individual's Will do exactly as they intended.
