What is Willcoin

Our goal is to automate the asset distrubution to an individual's beneficiaries when they pass away using smart living wills implemented using smart contracts. Upon passing, an individual's smart will automatically executes and assets are transferred to their designated beneficiaries. This allows individuals to skip the expensive and arduous court probate process.

Smart Living Wills

Willcoin leverages smart contract technology to create self-executing smart living wills that live on a distributed network allowing individuals to save on legal fees.

Automatic Assest Distribution

Willcoin helps individuals automatically distrbiute their personal assets to their designated beneficiaries anywhere in the world while decreasing undesired alterations.

Simplified Probate Process

Smart wills help the deceased perform the actions they planned upon their death. This removes the need for court probate processes, removes loopholes, and human error commonly found in written wills.

Technical Details

The idea for willcoin was born from personal experience. The father of one of our team members had passed and designated that his assets be transfered to his children. The process should have been a simple one, however due to some ambiguous wording in some parts of his will, it instead turned into a tedious and expensive seven month process. The family had to pay expensive court, attorney, bond, business valuation, and other miscellaneous fees. Instead of grieving the passing of their father, the family had to waste time and money in court. We thought this wasn't right so we made willcoin so that individuals and their families can find peace knowing that an individual's will will do exactly as they intended.

Current Court Process Self-Executing Wills
1 File a petition with probate court to admit the will and appoint an executor. Generate a smart living will on the willcoin distributed network.
2 Executor gathers assets of the estate and notifies creditors. Use willcoin to encode and execute your living will within the network.
3 All estate and funeral expenses must be paid from the estate. Receive a digital signature in order to view the smart contract containing the will.
4 Legal title is transferred per the will and testament. Once the deceased has passed, the smart automatically verifies and releases assets to the beneficiaries.
